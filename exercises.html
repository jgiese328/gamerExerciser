Stretching Basics

So you have come... to learn or expand upon your knowledge of stretching your arms/wrists. Please click on the following images to learn the steps to complete your training.


Prayer Stretch

Place your palms together centered in front of your chest and slowly lower your hands toward your waistline. Keep lowering until you feel a mild to moderate stretch under your forearms. Hold for 15 to 30 seconds. Repeat 4 times.

Wrist Flexor Stretch

Extend your arms in front of you with elbows straight, take the wrist of one arm and pull backwards using your other hand until you feel a mild to moderate stretch. Hold for 15-20 seconds and repeat 3 times.

Thumb Stretch

Start with your arm extended with palm up. keep fingers straight and bring thumb to the side the shape of an L or reverse L. Pull thumb back while bringing palm backwards toward the top of your forearm. Hold gently for 15-20 seconds. Repeat 3 times.

Side Wrist Stretch

Start with your left arm extended and bring your hand into a fist. Push down on your left hand with your right, hold the stretch for 15 seconds. Repeat on the opposite side.

Overhead Tricep Stretch

Lift you right arm above your head and let your hand fall behind your head. Grad your elbow with your left hand and pull, holding for 15 seconds. Switch arms and repeat the process.

Biceps Wall Stretch

Standing a few inches away from a sturdy wall, reach back with your right arm and place the palm, upper arm and the inside of your elbow flat against the wall. Gently turn your body to the opposite side until you feel a light stretch along your biceps. Hold for a count of 10 to 15 seconds, then switch sides.
