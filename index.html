Welcome Gamers!

The site you are on is devoted to keeping you at the top of your game, literally. Between offering stretching suggestions to prepare you for extended gaming sessions, to suggesting exercises for jump-starting a healthier routine, this site will give you the resources to get you there.


First Steps: Stretching

Ever get a cramp in your wrist or have pain in your arm that has stopped a gaming session? There are steps you can take to help prevent this from happening:

  • Simple stretches can prepare you for extended gaming sessions
  • Stretching can also help relieve existing wrist and arm cramps
  • Stretching can increase flexibility (PC GAMERS: this means a potential increase to keyboard accesibility)
  • Stretching can also prevent injury and the onset of carpal tunnel syndrom

Check out the 'Stretching Exercises' link to find out some specific stretches you can use to prevent injury.

Second Step: Challenge Yourself!


Why stick to only stretching? Go further by adding some daily aerobic and anaerobic exercises. Level up your daily exercise routine!

  • A good start would be to start small... Go out for a walk and do some basic arm exercises.
  • Build up more weekly or monthly. Go at your own pace. In gaming terms, level up!

Use this site's 'Going Further' page to get challenges you can use to improve your exercise level.

Third Step: Profit!

...errr... MEANT to say... Extended gaming sessions!

  • Through adding stretching exercises in particular, you can help prevent injuries and improve stamina.
  • More stamina and a lower injury rate means more games can be played during a larger block of time.

Check out my personal "Game Recommendations" to keep the cycle going. Above all, make sure when integrating stretching and exercises that you continue to have fun! Level up your health while leveling up as as gamer.
