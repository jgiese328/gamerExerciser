First Steps: Stretching

Ever get a cramp in your wrist or have pain in your arm that has stopped a gaming session? There are steps you can take to help prevent this from happening:

  • Simple stretches can prepare you for extended gaming sessions
  • Stretching can also help relieve existing cramping
  • Stretching can increase flexibility (PC GAMERS: this means potential increase to keyboard accesibility)
  • Stretching can also prevent injury and the onset of carpal tunnel syndrom
